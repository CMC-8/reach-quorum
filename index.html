The Quorum takes its name from the number whose presence makes a gathering complete. That is who we are: a community aligned in its ideals, measured in its actions, and united by the conviction to live by what we share.

Our Tenets

More than a membership — a community held together by what we believe and the standards we keep.

02

Commitment

To belong is to be counted. Lend your presence and hold the whole, and yourself, to the standards we keep.

03

Communion

When we convene, Quorum is met. Ideas, ideals, and common purpose are renewed together.
